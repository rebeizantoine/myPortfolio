JOBBLITZ

Your All-in-One Recruitment Platform

Deployed Link Video Demo

JOBBLITZ is a comprehensive recruitment platform where job seekers can create profiles, browse jobs, and apply, while employers can post job listings and recruit top talent. Both job seekers and employers have fully customizable profiles and dashboards.

Project Overview

JOBBLITZ is designed to simplify the recruitment process for both job seekers and employers. It features customizable profiles, real-time job postings, and an intuitive dashboard for easy management.

The platform offers the following key features:

  • Job Seekers: Create a profile, browse jobs, and apply directly.
  • Employers: Post jobs, view applicants, and recruit top talent.
  • Customizable Profiles: Job seekers and employers can fully customize their profile pages.
  • Dashboard: Manage job postings, user data, and privacy policies with ease.
