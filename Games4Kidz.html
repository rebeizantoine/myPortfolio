Games4Kidz

Fun & Educational Games for Kids

Deployed Link Video Demo

Discover fun and educational games for kids! Games4Kidz offers interactive learning experiences, helping kids improve math skills, learn shapes, and identify animals.

Project Overview

Games4Kidz is a platform offering various games to make learning engaging for children. The site includes:

MathKidz: A math game to improve addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division skills.

  • ShapeForKidz: Helps kids recognize and match different shapes through interactive play.
  • Animal4Kidz: Teaches kids to identify animals and learn fun facts about them.
  • Kids Quiz: An interactive quiz to test kids' knowledge in various subjects.
  • Dashboard: A feature-rich dashboard for managing orders filtering prices and data/time of the purchase.
