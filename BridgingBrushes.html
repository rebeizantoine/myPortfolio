Bridging Brushes

Showcasing Artists, Artworks, and Exhibitions

Deployed Link Video Demo

Bridging Brushes is a platform designed to connect art enthusiasts with talented artists, exclusive artworks, and curated exhibitions.

Project Overview

Bridging Brushes offers a comprehensive platform for showcasing and exploring art, featuring tools to enhance the artist and viewer experience. Visitors can discover new artists, browse unique artworks, and stay informed about upcoming exhibitions.

The site features a comprehensive suite of tools to enhance your shopping experience, including:

  • Artist Profiles: Detailed pages for artists to showcase their work and background.
  • Artworks: A curated collection of art from various mediums and styles.
  • Exhibitions: Browse current, past, and future exhibitions with all relevant details.
  • Dashboard: A feature-rich dashboard for managing artist profiles, artworks, and exhibitions.
